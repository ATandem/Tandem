Chuchu

Attack: 2

HP: 3(small) 20(medium) 48(big)

Drops:

Chuchu Jelly

These are pretty useful. You can combine the Chuchu jelly with elemental weapons and arrows to create the colored ones, which are very useful in armor upgrades

Where:

They can be found anywhere in Hyrule, mostly in fields.

In Game Description

This low-level, gel-based monster can be found all over Hyrule. It tends to spring its attacks on unsuspecting prey from the ground or from trees. Its strength varies by size, and the type of jelly it drops varies depending on whether the Chuchu was heated up, cooled down, or shocked.
