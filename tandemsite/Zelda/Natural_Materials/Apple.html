Apple

Apples have no special effects.

Hearts Recovered when eaten alone: 1/2

Hearts Recovered when dropped on a plain fire: 1

Apples are the most common and average fruits you will find in Hyrule. They're kinda useful when fighting very weak enemies.

Where:

They can be found anywhere in Hyrule; on trees, in barrels, in crates, or bought.

In Game Description

A common fruit found on trees all around Hyrule. Eat it fresh, or cook it to increase its effect.
